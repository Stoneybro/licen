# Quickstart

Get started with LICEN as a dataset provider or an AI researcher in just a few minutes. 

Since LICEN operates on the **0G Testnet**, you will need a Web3 wallet and some testnet tokens to interact with the platform.

## Prerequisites

1.  **A Web3 Wallet:** We recommend [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/).
2.  **Add the 0G Testnet to your Wallet:**
    *   **Network Name:** 0G Testnet
    *   **RPC URL:** `https://evmrpc-testnet.0g.ai`
    *   **Chain ID:** `16602`
    *   **Currency Symbol:** `0G`
    *   **Block Explorer:** `https://scan-testnet.0g.ai`
3.  **Get Testnet Tokens:** Visit the [0G Faucet](https://faucet.0g.ai/) and request testnet tokens for your wallet address. You will need these to pay for storage and compute fees.

---

## Path 1: Publish a Dataset

If you have data you want to monetize:

1.  **Format your data:** Prepare your dataset in `JSONL` format. This is the standard format required for fine-tuning on 0G Compute.
    ```json
    {"messages": [{"role": "system", "content": "You are a helpful assistant."}, {"role": "user", "content": "Hello!"}, {"role": "assistant", "content": "Hi there!"}]}
    ```
2.  **Connect to LICEN:** Go to the LICEN application and connect your Web3 wallet.
3.  **Navigate to Publish:** Click on "Publish Dataset".
4.  **Upload & Encrypt:** Select your `JSONL` file. The file will be encrypted locally in your browser.
5.  **Set Policies:** Define the cost per epoch and the maximum number of epochs researchers can train on your data.
6.  **Confirm Transaction:** Approve the transaction in your wallet. The encrypted dataset will be uploaded to 0G Storage, and your policy will be registered on-chain.

---

## Path 2: Fine-tune a Model

If you are a researcher looking to train a model:

1.  **Browse the Marketplace:** Connect your wallet and navigate to the LICEN marketplace to browse available datasets.
2.  **Select a Dataset:** Click on a dataset to view its details, including the price per epoch.
3.  **Configure Compute:** Choose how many epochs you want to train for. The total cost will be calculated automatically.
4.  **Purchase Access:** Click "Start Fine-Tuning" and approve the transaction in your wallet. This transfers the payment to the dataset provider and grants you access.
5.  **Track the Job:** Navigate to your "Sessions" or "Dashboard" page. You will see the job move from `Queue` -> `Running` -> `Completed`. The LICEN Orchestrator handles the background dispatch automatically.
6.  **Download the Model:** Once completed, you will receive a link to download the resulting trained model (LoRA adapter) from 0G Storage.
