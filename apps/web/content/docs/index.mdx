# What is LICEN?

**LICEN** is a decentralized marketplace and infrastructure platform that bridges the gap between secure data storage and verifiable AI compute.

It solves a fundamental problem in the modern AI ecosystem: **How can dataset creators safely monetize their data for AI training without losing control of it, and how can AI researchers access high-quality datasets seamlessly?**

## The Problem

1. **For Data Creators:** Publishing a dataset often means losing ownership. Once data is public, anyone can use it to train models for free.
2. **For AI Researchers:** Finding, purchasing, and moving high-quality datasets into compute environments is a fragmented, expensive, and manual process.

## The LICEN Solution

LICEN provides an end-to-end pipeline that connects **Data Providers** directly to **AI Researchers** using blockchain technology, cryptographic security, and decentralized infrastructure.

### Key Features

*   **In-Browser Encryption:** Datasets are encrypted with AES directly in the user's browser. The plaintext data never touches LICEN's servers.
*   **Decentralized Storage:** Encrypted datasets are stored on the **0G Storage Network**, ensuring permanent, censorship-resistant availability.
*   **Smart Contract Access Control:** Access to decrypt the dataset is governed by an on-chain `DataPolicy` smart contract. Researchers must pay the required fee to gain access.
*   **Automated AI Compute:** Once a researcher purchases access, the LICEN **Orchestrator** automatically unseals the data and dispatches a fine-tuning job to the **0G Compute Network**.
*   **Verifiable Output:** The researcher receives the final trained model (e.g., a LoRA adapter) directly, without ever having to download or manually process the underlying dataset.

By leveraging the [0G Foundation's](https://0g.ai/) infrastructure, LICEN creates a trustless environment where data is respected, and compute is automated.

## Who is this for?

*   **Dataset Providers:** Monetize your proprietary datasets securely.
*   **AI Researchers & Engineers:** Fine-tune models (like Qwen2.5) effortlessly without managing infrastructure.
